Enjoy in Busan

  1. Gwangalli Beach
    2. Gwangalli Beach is one of the famous beach in Busan. Every August, festivals is held in this beach as well as Busan Firework Festival. The ocean water is clean and cold; therefore, great place to visit and enjoy during summer. Gwangalli Beach at night is also beautiful. The lights flashed out from the bridge and buildings lighten up the beach.

  2. Haeundae Beach
    3. Haeundae Beach is also one of the famous beach in Busan. The difference between Gwangalli Beach and Haundae Beach is that there is no bridge in Haeundae Beach. While Gwangalli Beach is known for swimming and family vacation, Haeundae Beach is more for adults who wants to play and enjoy the night. There lots of liquor shop, restaurants, and people who enjoy talking and hanging out. Aside from that, Haeundae Beach is beautiful with strong waves and wide view of sky and ocean that calms you down and removes stress.
