Enjoy in Seoul

  1. Lotte World
    2. Lotte World is one of the biggest amusement parks in South Korea. It's famous for great view, fun roller coasters, merry-go-round, and delicious, tasty food. One of its trademark is definitely the castle located in the middle of the amusement park. It is beautiful at night and great place to take pictures. Especially in holidays like Christmas or Halloween, Lotte World host events by decorating the whole amusement park, makeups, and fireworks in the castle.

  2. Palace
    3. Seoul has five palaces where past royal families lived in: Gyeongbokgung Palace, Changdeokgung Palace, Changgyeonggung Palace, Deoksugung Palace, and Gyeonghui Palace. These five palaces have been remained well that you could see how past royal families lived and maintained their loyal and position. They are beauitful; you could see the architecture structure in past South Korea. There are also hosts who explain each part of palaces (also in English), so great place to learn Korea history.
