Enjoy in Jeju-Island

  1. Hamo Beach
    2. Hamo Beach is one of the beach in Jeju-Island located in the southern side. It is local beach where not many tourists visit. Because of this reason, you'll be able to enjoy local beach like other Jeju citizens. Because of nice weather in Jeju, Hamo Beach is known for sunrise. In here, you can see beautiful sunrise in the morning, rising from the border of the ocean line. The water is clean and cool, and wave is strong because of the wind. It's best for dog to swim.

  2. Oiljang (Five-day Market)
    3. Oiljang is a Korean market that opens after five days from the date they open; so, it is first opens on first day of the month, then on the sixth, then on eleventh, and so on. From here, you could enjoy local food, such as Tteokbokki (Korean spicy ricecake), Hoteok (Korean sweet honey bread), and other fresh vegetables in cheap prices. Even Koreans visit here because the food in here tastes different than any other place.
