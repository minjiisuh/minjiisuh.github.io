South Korea

is a country located in East Asia. Its three sides of land is surrounded by ocean, neighboring North Korea, Japan, and China. The territory of South Korea is small with the fact that Korea has divided into two. Despite the fact that it's small country, South Korea has developed successfully with lots of great places and sights to see and enjoy.
