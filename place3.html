Jeju-Island

Jeju-Island is an island located in the south of South Korea: more south than Busan. It's the biggest island in South Korea. As you can tell by the name, Jeju is surrounded by ocean. There are beautiful well-known beach, such as Hyupjae Beach, Hamdeok Beach, or Gwakji Beach. The colours of oceans are aquagreen; beautiful and clean. The weather is nice, mostly sunny and windy everyday. Jeju is also a region where people speak dialect. The food in Jeju is very unique as it is located far from the main land. Very interesting and fun place to visit.
